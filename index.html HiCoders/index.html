Swakopmund Namibia

Swakopmund meaning (Germam:Mouth of Swkop) was founded by Captain Kurt Von Francious
in 1892 as the main harbour for Imperial German Colony. Swakopmund is a beach Resort situated
352km from the Capital City of Windhoek.

Activies to do in Swakopmund

  1. Visiting the Wooden Jetty Completed in 1905
  2. Visiting the delightfull German-Style Woermanhaus
  3. The Hohenzollerhaus build as a hote in Neo Baraque in 1904-1906 This building is now a comdomimium
  4. Swkopmund lighthouse, was used to guide ships into Swakopmund, and is a landmark which enjoys international prominence.
